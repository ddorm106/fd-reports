Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a room?

1. Tap "New Building Scan" on the home screen

2. Enter the building name, address, and floor number

3. Point your camera at the room and slowly pan around

4. The app uses Apple‚Äôs RoomPlan to detect walls, doors, windows, and furniture

5. When finished, tap "Complete Scan" to generate the floor plan

Tip: Move slowly and steadily. Keep the phone upright. Scan from corners for best results.

What are fire service markers?

Markers are icons you place on the floor plan to document fire protection equipment and hazards. DivisionScan includes 30+ pre-built markers:

  • Fire Protection: FDC, sprinkler risers, standpipes, pull stations, extinguishers, smoke/CO detectors
  • Electrical: Panels, outlets, switches, lights, exit signs, emergency lights
  • Utilities: Gas/water/electrical shutoffs, HVAC, elevators, generators
  • Hazards: HazMat storage, flammables, chemicals, oxygen, propane
  • Access: Knox boxes, exits, stairwells, elevators, roof access
How do I add photos to the floor plan?

1. On the floor plan view, tap the camera icon in the toolbar

2. Tap a location on the floor plan where you want to pin the photo

3. Take a photo or select from your camera roll

4. Add an optional note to describe what the photo shows

Photos are pinned to exact locations on the floor plan, making it easy to reference during an incident.
