DIVISIONSCAN
Privacy Policy
Effective Date: February 7, 2026

Summary: DivisionScan processes all data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to any external servers unless you explicitly choose to export scan data.

1. Information We Collect

Data Collected Automatically

DivisionScan does not automatically collect any personal information. The app does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking frameworks.

Data You Create

The app allows you to create the following data, all stored locally on your device:

  • Building scans: 3D room scans captured using your device’s LiDAR sensor, converted to 2D floor plans
  • Building information: Names, addresses, construction details, and hazard notes you enter manually
  • Photos: Images captured through the in-app camera, stored in the app’s sandboxed storage
  • Markers and annotations: Fire protection markers, hand-drawn annotations, and notes you place on floor plans

Device Permissions

DivisionScan requests the following device permissions:

  • Camera & LiDAR: Required for scanning building interiors and photo documentation
  • Location (When In Use): Used solely for compass heading calibration to orient floor plans to true north. Your GPS coordinates are not recorded or transmitted.
  • Face ID / Touch ID: Optional biometric authentication for the app lock feature. Biometric data is handled entirely by iOS and never accessed by the app.

2. How Your Data Is Used

All scan data is processed entirely on your device using Apple’s RoomPlan framework. Your data is used only for the purposes you direct:

  • Generating and displaying 2D floor plans from 3D LiDAR scans
  • Saving scans locally for later reference
  • Exporting scan data when you explicitly choose to share, AirDrop, or send to a server

3. Data Sharing and Export

DivisionScan only transmits data when you explicitly initiate an export action:

  • AirDrop: Sends scan data directly to nearby Apple devices
  • Share Sheet: Exports JSON or USDZ files through iOS’s standard sharing interface
  • Server Export: If you choose to send scan data to a department server, data is transmitted to that specific URL only.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. There are no advertising networks, analytics services, or data brokers involved.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in the app’s sandboxed storage on your device, protected by iOS’s built-in security:

  • Scan files are stored in the app’s Documents directory
  • Photos are stored in the app’s private photo storage
  • PIN codes are stored in iOS Keychain (encrypted)
  • No data is stored in iCloud or any cloud service by the app

When you delete the app, all associated data is permanently removed from your device.

5. Children’s Privacy

DivisionScan is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the “Effective Date” at the top of this page.
